Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide enaging information online to promote the thrilling adventures offered by our white water rafting business. Our goal is to attract and convert potential customers by highlighting our expertise, safety measures, and the unique experiences we provide.

Target Audience

Our website targets adventure seekers - individuals and groups - between the ages of 18 and 45. This includes university students, young professionals and families with middle to upper-middle-class incomes. Our audience seeks memorable whiterafting experiences that are safe, well-organized and led by expert guides. They value unique outdoor activities, opportunities to bond with loved ones and connecting with nature.

Typically, our audience will access our website via mobile devices, though they may also use desktops or laptops for detailed planning and booking.
