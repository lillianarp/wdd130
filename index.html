New Zealand

At the bottom of the Pacific ocean, around 2000 kilometers southeast of Australia, New Zealand is one of the most isolated countries in the world. It's known for its stunning landscapes and unique flora and fauna, and offers a diverse range of experiences for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. Explore the geothermal pools of Rotorua or sail the breathtaking fjords of Milford Sound; go bungee jumping in Queenstown or whale watching in Kaikoura; sample the Hobbit life in our Hobbiton Shire, or soak up Pacific culture in the big city of Auckland - it's all here in New Zealand.
